    Wall size

    Large-format photo posters (e.g. Copy General fotoplakaty) are printed borderless, so the printer margins below don't apply.

    Printer

    Unprintable margins of your printer (mm). Print the calibration sheet at 100 % scale to measure them.

    Map content

    Higher detail exports at a deeper map zoom: more streets, names and buildings on every page, at the cost of smaller print. The resulting zoom is shown under Print preview.

    Print preview

    –

    The PDF contains exactly the features the map shows at the print zoom above — not at whatever zoom you inspect with. Lock the area, then zoom to the print zoom to preview the content of a page; raise Detail if streets or labels are missing at that zoom.

    Output

    Copy centres usually take JPEG/PNG directly; one file per page, named by its grid position (R1C1…). The optional grid is spaced 10 cm in printed size and continues across sheets — measure it to verify scale, look at the hairlines to judge sharpness.
